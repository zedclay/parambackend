## Notes

- All programs follow the "Licence Professionnalisante" format (3-year programs)
- All programs are specifically for "Santé Publique" (Public Health)
- Programs are organized under "Formation initiale" (Initial Training)
- The institute also offers "Formation continue" (Continuing Education)
